# Tax Knowledge Diffusion via Strategic Alliances
Online Repository for the Research Article "Tax Knowledge Diffusion via Strategic Alliances"

Version: 08/19/2020

Highlights:
- Strategic alliances serve as channels for tax knowledge diffusion between firms 
- Low-tax networks cause increases in tax planning behavior of high-tax firms
- Effects do not materialize before two years after a network's initiation
- Textual analyses of deal texts, 10-K filings, and Exhibit 21 corroborate effects
